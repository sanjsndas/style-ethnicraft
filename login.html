Our Customer Satisfaction Promise

At StyleEthniCraft, we stand behind every product in our collection. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange. We work directly with artisans to ensure the highest quality standards, but in the rare case an item arrives damaged or doesn't meet your expectations, we'll make it right. Our team is available 7 days a week to answer questions about products, help with styling suggestions, or assist with any concerns. We believe in the beauty and quality of our handcrafted tableware and want you to love every piece as much as we do.

Delivery times may vary based on location and customs processing. We carefully package all items to withstand international shipping, but please inspect your order upon arrival and contact us immediately if there are any issues.
